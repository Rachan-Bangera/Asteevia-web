About ASTEEVIA

Asteevia 2025 is a prestigious intercollegiate fest organized by the Department of BCA and BSC at Sri Mahaveera College. It serves as a vibrant platform for students to showcase their talents and compete in a diverse range of events. The fest is designed to bring together creativity and innovation, providing opportunities to test knowledge, teamwork, problem-solving abilities, and coding skills. Asteevia 2025 offers challenges that inspire innovation and strategic thinking. It is not just a competition, but also an opportunity for participants to gain new insights, collaborate with peers, build lasting relationships, and enhance their professional competencies in a stimulating environment.
